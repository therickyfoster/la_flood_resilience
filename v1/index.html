Altadena / Eaton Burn Scar

Altitude: 1,200-2,400 ft | Risk: Critical | Active Orders: 383+ properties

üöß K-Rail Barrier Deployment Immediate

Concrete Jersey barriers to divert debris flows away from structures. Prioritize downslope properties at canyon mouths.

  • Survey debris flow channels from burn scar
  • Position K-rails perpendicular to expected flow direction
  • Ensure minimum 100ft continuous barrier per critical structure
  • Anchor with sandbags at connection points
  • Leave drainage gaps to prevent pooling

üé™ Hillside Tarp Coverage Immediate

Plastic sheeting over exposed burn scar soil to reduce erosion and runoff velocity.

  • Identify steepest exposed slopes above structures
  • Deploy 20ft x 100ft plastic sheets
  • Secure with wood stakes (17,790 available from DWR)
  • Overlap sheets by minimum 2ft
  • Direct runoff to designated drainage channels

Erosion control tubes placed along contours to slow runoff and filter sediment.
